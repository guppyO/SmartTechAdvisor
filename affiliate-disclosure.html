Affiliate Disclosure

This Affiliate Disclosure explains how Smart Tech Advisor ("we," "us," or "our") participates in affiliate marketing programs and earns commissions through the links on our website.

What Is Affiliate Marketing?

Affiliate marketing is a way for websites to earn commissions by promoting products or services of other companies. When you click on an affiliate link on our site and make a purchase, we may receive a small commission from that sale, at no additional cost to you.

Our Affiliate Relationships

Smart Tech Advisor participates in various affiliate programs, including but not limited to:

  • Amazon Associates Program: As an Amazon Associate, we earn from qualifying purchases when you use our links to shop on Amazon.com and related sites.
  • Best Buy Affiliate Program
  • Walmart Affiliate Program
  • eBay Partner Network
  • Newegg Affiliate Program
  • Other retailer and manufacturer affiliate programs

How to Identify Affiliate Links

Affiliate links on our site may be identified in several ways:

  • Links labeled as "View Deal," "Buy Now," or similar call-to-action text
  • Product showcase sections with pricing information
  • Links within product reviews and comparison articles
  • Some text links within articles that mention specific products

Additionally, all Amazon links will typically contain a tag that looks like "?tag=youraffiliateid-20" or similar in the URL.

Our Commitment to You

We want to emphasize the following important points about our affiliate relationships:

  • Editorial Independence: Our product recommendations and reviews are based on our genuine opinions and research. We never recommend a product solely to earn a commission.
  • No Additional Cost: Using our affiliate links does not cost you anything extra. Prices are the same as if you visited the retailer's site directly.
  • No Influence on Content: Retailers and manufacturers do not influence our content or have any input on our reviews or recommendations.
  • Transparency: We are committed to being transparent about our affiliate relationships, which is why we've created this disclosure page.

How We Select Products to Recommend

Our product recommendations are based on a combination of:

  • Hands-on testing (where possible)
  • Technical specifications and features
  • Customer reviews and feedback
  • Brand reputation and reliability
  • Price and value for money
  • Comparative analysis with similar products

While we do earn commissions from affiliate links, this never impacts our product ratings or recommendations. If we don't believe in a product, we won't recommend it, regardless of commission potential.

FTC Compliance Statement

This disclosure is provided in accordance with the Federal Trade Commission's 16 CFR § 255: "Guides Concerning the Use of Endorsements and Testimonials in Advertising." We aim to be fully transparent with our audience about the commercial relationships that support our website.
